About

For over a decade at Bosch, I led product strategy and operations for safety- and availability-critical systems in continuous manufacturing—including industrial process steam, thermal energy, and power generation solutions—where reliability, compliance, and uptime are non-negotiable.

At Bosch, I managed IoT and B2B SaaS portfolios spanning hardware, embedded controls, cloud software, and advanced analytics. I identified gaps in existing offerings, translated customer and market signals into high-performing products, and delivered portfolios that improved adoption, generated sustainable revenue growth, and supported long-term competitiveness.

Today, as Interim Principal PM at Virtus Labs, I'm leading product development of Eugene AI—a full-stack agentic orchestration layer that autonomously manages multi-project coordination, personal & professional task planning, and content creation. It's both a functional product and a portfolio demonstration of end-to-end PM execution: market research, PRDs, technical architecture, database design, and go-to-market strategy.

I also partner with SaaS companies and technology-driven organizations to apply AI and ML, helping leaders evaluate portfolios, prioritize initiatives, and translate advanced capabilities into solutions that deliver measurable outcomes.

What drives me? The challenge of turning "what if" into "what's next."
